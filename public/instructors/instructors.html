INSTRUCTORS & STAFF

Stepping Stones Preschool has two classroom teachers each day in addition to one parent from the Cooperative Plan assisting to ensuring an average adult/child ratio of 1:6.

NANCY LILIENTHAL

Nancy has been the Director at Stepping Stones Preschool since 1995 and continues as Lead Teacher for the Monday/Wednesday/Friday class. She has a B.S. degree in Child Development with an Early Childhood Accreditation from ISU. She is licensed to teach preschool and kindergarten in Iowa and is a member of the National Association of the Education for Young Children (NAEYC) and the Iowa AEYC. She has met the requirements for the National Administrator Credential from the National Child Care Association. Nancy's 25+ year history with Stepping Stones as a parent, board member, and teacher has been an asset to our school. She has three of her children, who all attended Stepping Stones, and two grandchildren.

SHARON GOOD

Sharon comes to us as a Stepping Stones parent and former elementary music teacher. We are delighted to have her as the Tuesday/Thursday lead teacher. She has a B.M. in Music Education from the University of Northern Iowa and has experience substitute teaching K-12 classes for the ICCSD. Sharon enjoys incorporating music and movement into teaching. Her warm, caring personality and desire to see students learn and grow are a great benefit to the school. In her free time she likes to cook, craft, exercise, and drink coffee. She lives in Iowa City with her husband and three children.

LAURIE WYATT

Laurie has experience as an educator, performer and freelance writer, in addition to being the parent of a Stepping Stones Preschool graduate. Prior to her work with us, she was a Supervisory Paraeducator at Lincoln Elementary where she primarily worked with Kindergarten students. She holds a B.A. from the University of Northern Iowa in Performance/Theatre and is a member of the National Association for the Education of Young Children and the Iowa AEYC. She brings great enthusiasm and creativity with her to Stepping Stones.

CATE BURKE

Cate started with Stepping Stones Preschool as the Board Treasurer and then joined the staff as School Administrator when her twins started Kindergarten in 2013. She has extensive experience working with non-profit organizations in the areas of administration, public relations, marketing, and development. She received her B.A. in Communication from San Diego State University. In addition to her work as Stepping Stones, she is also a substitute for the Iowa City Community School District.
