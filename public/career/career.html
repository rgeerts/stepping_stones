Preschool Director and Lead Teacher

Stepping Stones Preschool in Iowa City is searching for an enthusiastic teacher to provide high quality education for 3-5 year olds beginning Fall 2017. This position requires an Iowa teaching certificate and Early Childhood Endorsement.

Stepping Stones Preschool is a non-profit, cooperative preschool that is affiliated with the Iowa City Community School District and receives funding from the SWVPP. Classes are M-F half-day, mornings. Stepping Stones recently achieved a 4 Star rating for the Iowa Quality Rating System!

Salary is commensurate with education and experience. Please send resume and cover letter to stepping.stones.iowacity@gmail.com
